Concept Traceability

Each brief concept should resolve to work that can be assigned.

This page keeps the strategy-to-execution line visible. The brief explains why the DMS should exist; the workstreams define what must be built, decided, validated, or migrated.

Highest Leverage Concepts

These concepts drive the largest implementation decisions.

01 Source of Truth GitHub becomes the governed record, not just a collaboration tool.

Impacts repository structure, migration readiness, publication controls, and audit evidence.

02 Approval Parity PoP tollgates must remain enforceable after LogicGate replacement.

Impacts branch protection, CODEOWNERS, issue signoff, QC gates, and exception paths.

03 Two Surfaces Employees get a simple portal; governance teams get controlled GitHub workflows.

Impacts portal scope, user roles, training, support model, and search experience.
